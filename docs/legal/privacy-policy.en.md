# Privacy Policy — Huismus

**Last updated:** 18 July 2026

Huismus (“we”, “us”) is an app and website for discovering homes for sale in the Netherlands on a map, with neighborhood insights based on public statistics. This policy explains what data we process, why, and what rights you have. It's written to match what the app actually does — not what's typical for similar apps.

**Data controller:** Fastvibes, email support@huismusapp.com.

## 1. The short version

- We don't build a profile of you, and we never sell or rent your data to third parties.
- Your searches, favorites, recently viewed listings, and filter settings stay **on your device** — we don't send them to our servers.
- We only measure anonymous, cookie-free app usage to see which features get used. You can turn this off in Settings.
- If you create an account, we only store your email address and name to make that account work.

## 2. What data we process

### 2.1 Account data
If you sign in with Google or create an account with an email address, we receive and store your **email address** and (if available via Google) your **name**. This is needed to make your account work and to recognize you on your next visit. When signing in with an email address, your password is stored encrypted; we cannot read it in plain text.

Your session is secured with a temporary access token (JWT). On phones and tablets this token is kept in the operating system's secure keychain; on the web version it's kept in your browser's local storage, since a browser has no keychain.

### 2.2 Data that stays on your device only
The following data is **stored locally on your device** (not sent to our servers), unless you explicitly opt into a feature that does:

- Favorited listings
- Recently viewed listings
- Search filters and preferences (e.g. price range, number of rooms, selected cities)
- Recent searches
- Language and appearance settings (light/dark)
- Whether you've already seen the intro tour

If we ever link this data to your account so it follows you to another device, we'll update this policy before that feature goes live.

### 2.3 Anonymous usage analytics
We measure anonymized, cookie-free app usage (e.g. which screens get visited) via a self-hosted analytics service (Plausible). These measurements contain no name, email address, stored IP address, or other personally identifiable data, and are never used to track or profile you individually. You can turn this off entirely in **Settings → Privacy & security**.

### 2.4 Feedback you send us
If you use the feedback form, we receive the message you type, and (so we can reproduce technical issues) your app version, platform (iOS/Android/web), and app language. We use this only to handle your feedback.

### 2.5 Listing data shown in the app
The property listings you see in Huismus come from public and/or third-party real estate sources. This is not data *about you* — it's information about homes and (where listed) the offering party. We don't guarantee this data is current, complete, or accurate; see also our Terms of Use.

### 2.6 Neighborhood statistics
The neighborhood insights in the app (e.g. about residents, income, or energy usage) are based on public, aggregated statistics from Statistics Netherlands (CBS) and similar public sources. These are statistics at the neighborhood or district level, not personal data about individual residents.

## 3. Why we process this data

| Data | Purpose | Legal basis (GDPR) |
|---|---|---|
| Account data | Making your account work, recognizing you when you sign in | Performance of a contract |
| Session token | Keeping you signed in, securing your account | Performance of a contract |
| Anonymous usage analytics | Improving the app based on what's actually used | Legitimate interest |
| Feedback message | Handling your question or report | Legitimate interest / performance of a contract |
| Listing data, neighborhood statistics | The app's core function: showing and comparing homes | Performance of a contract |

We never use your data for automated decision-making with legal effects on you, and we never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Cookies and similar technologies

The Huismus app doesn't use tracking cookies. Our usage analytics are cookie-free. The web version uses only technically necessary local storage (e.g. to keep you signed in) — no advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

## 5. Who we share data with

- **Hosting provider:** our servers and database run with a hosting provider that acts solely as a processor and doesn't use data for its own purposes.
- **Google:** if you sign in with Google, Google processes the sign-in data needed to confirm your identity, under Google's own privacy policy.
- **Our anonymous analytics** run on infrastructure we manage ourselves — not shared with an external advertising or tracking party.

We don't work with ad networks, data brokers, or third-party tracking pixels.

## 6. Transfers outside the EEA

Wherever possible, we process and store data within the European Economic Area (EEA). Should a processor process data outside the EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards, such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

## 7. Retention periods

| Data | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Account data | For as long as your account exists; deleted after account deletion |
| Session token | Until you sign out or the token expires |
| Anonymous usage analytics | Aggregated, not traceable to an individual — no retention period applies |
| Feedback message | As long as needed to handle your report, then deleted |
| Data stored locally on your device | Until you uninstall the app or clear the data yourself |

## 8. Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

- **request access** to the data we process about you;
- have inaccurate data **corrected**;
- have your data **deleted** (the "right to be forgotten");
- have processing **restricted**;
- receive your data in a structured format (**data portability**);
- **object** to processing based on legitimate interest;
- **withdraw** any consent you previously gave.

You can delete your account and its data yourself via **Settings**, or send a request to support@huismusapp.com. We respond within one month. If you disagree with how we handle your request, you can file a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens, autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl) or your local supervisory authority.

## 9. Children

Huismus is not directed at children under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from children under this age. If you believe we have, please contact us at support@huismusapp.com and we'll delete it.

## 10. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted storage of passwords, and restricted access to personal data within our team. No method of data transmission or storage can be guaranteed 100% secure, however.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy, for example when the app gains new features. For significant changes, we'll let you know via the app. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.
